Endless Neverland



Ya disponible en tapa blanda, en Kindle y otros tipos de E-books.
La historia que causó una revolución entre los cuenta cuentos de Tik Tok llega en una obra original en inglés y pronto en español.

“All children, except one, grow up”. With this words is how Peter Pan’s story begins, but this is not another Peter´s story. After the Darling siblings returned to London, Peter and Wendy made a deal: when spring break starts, Peter will take her to Neverland for all the month (just one). Everything was perfect for Wendy, but Peter didn´t see it in the same way When Wendy comes back to London after the last cleaning spring, her little brother has become an old man and her family grew old waiting for her. What she thought was an accident, was actually a plan made by the only one who knows how time works in Neverland.
